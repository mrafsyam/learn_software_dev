### Job Description
Software QA, Test Engineer (Expert/Intermediate/Beginner) positions for Multi National Company (Energy Sector) - 10 pax

### Role Responsibility
- Identifying, developing, executing, improving and maintaining the required tests (manual/automated), required for web and mobile applications
- Evaluate outcome of each test cycle
- Analyze the impact of new solutions
- Collaborate with other Test Engineer and Software Engineer to ensure that tests are developed according to our standards and best practices

- Adopt and practice Agile/Scrum within SDLC

### Role Requirement
- 3 years of experience in software testing, OR 1 year experience for Beginner role
- Experience in Automation Testing tools and Frameworks such as Katalon, Robot, Selenium, Appium
- Experience in Testing tools and Technologies such as Microsoft Team Foundation Server (TFS), Microsoft Azure Devops, MicroFocus / HP Application Lifecycle Management (ALM), MicroFocus / HP Unified Functional Testing (UFT) or others
- Experience in Performance testing, utilizing tools such as LoadRunner, JMeter or others; OR knowledge of Performance Testing for Beginner role
- Experience in any of the following language: Net/C#, Java, Python
- Experience in databases, primarily in writing SQL queries such as Microsoft SQL, Oracle or other similar databases
- Knowledge of Agile Scrum framework
- Experience with Cloud development (Azure, AWS) and DevOps environment
